# Code of Conduct

This project is intended to be welcoming, constructive, and focused on useful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Give feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about problems.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Spam or repeated off-topic disruption

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participation when behavior is harmful to the project or its contributors.

To report a concern, contact the maintainer privately through the security contact listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
